PRINCIPAL

Dr A V Prathap Kumar - With rich academic and industrial experience has been leading BITS in its endeavour to excel in imparting value based education with highest quality and integrity. After graduating in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Bundelkhand University, Jhansi, he completed Post Graduation from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam and obtained PhD from Andhra University, Vishakapatnam. He has vast and enduring experience as professor, principal, member of various academic and administrative bodies like Indian Society for Technical Education

He has attended, organized and chaired various conferences, seminars of national and international importance. He has published several analytical articles in various International & National besides presenting a few papers in Conferences of International and National importance. He has Published books in Electronics and Communication Engineering. He has published 30 papers in National and International Journals.

Message

Brindavan Institute of Technology & Science, Kurnool has, over the years, grown into an institution of greater repute and promise. Ever since its inception in the year 2008, under the guidance of many stalwarts in engineering and academic fields, this institution has been on the forefront to establish a honest and disciplined work culture with a definite intent to achieve success in every field.

There is no short-cut to success; and efforts have to be relentless and; the win situation to an institution would be a certainty if only there is transparency of work at every stage and commitment of the faculty members towards the future of the young generations. Every individual has to play a vital role with dedication and unchartered readiness. Today Brindavan Institute of Technology & Science, Kurnool has earned a name for itself, as the institution where discipline coupled with value based education is the main criterion. Once a reputation is attained, it is even a greater responsibility to hold on to the reputation and I am very happy that we at BITS Kurnool, as it is popularly known among the stakeholders, have been sincerely working to safe-guard this reputation and to scale newer heights of excellence in technical education. I believe that our students will turn out to be the best technocrats with strong human and ethical values.
