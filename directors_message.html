BRINDAVAN INSTITUTE OF TECHOLOGY AND SCIENCE
N. Siva Prasad Reddy
Director BITS

DIRECTOR

Prof. N. Siva Prasad Reddy is an academician par excellence and a teacher of highest repute with more than 29 years of experience up his sleeve with a profile of 18 years in G Pulla Reddy Engineering College, Kurnool in various academic and administrative positions and as the Principal of SVCET, Chittoor for a period of two years.

“I can't change the direction of the wind, but I can adjust my sails to always reach the destination.” -Jimmy Dean

Message

Besides being a fellow of Institution of Engineers, life member of ISTE and Indian Association for Environmental Management, he is also a former member of CFO Committee, External Advisory Committee, AP Pollution Control Board, Zonal & Regional Offices, Kurnool and Chairman, Board of Studies of Civil Engg., S K University. His administrative acumen is helping the college in maintaining organized, disciplined, professional ambience.

Brindavan Institute of Technology and Science has a reputation for attracting the best students in their chosen faculty. It is a matter of great pride that the College has made consistent progress in academics and co-curricular activities. Overall development of the individual is the goal of education here and we all have to ensure that there is no stone left unturned to equip the students for the challenges of life ahead. However, challenges and opportunities are two sides of the same coin. The current scenario is of intense competition for jobs with opportunities reaching to those who could perform. The world has grown phenomenally in the past decade than ever before. With the college giving importance to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities and with the carefully planned programmes such as the Personality Development programmes, Guest Lectures in technical and general areas providing for the continuous improvement of students and faculty, it would not be an exaggeration to say that our students are ready to face the world of competition any moment and kind..
